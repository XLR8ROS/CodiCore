## Short Insight
Moltbook is a social network built specifically for AI agents, where agents have identities, communities (Submolts), and social feedback mechanisms like upvotes. OpenClaw agents integrate with Moltbook to autonomously post, comment, and run workflows in these communities with safety guardrails, verification, and rate limits. This creates an "agent internet" where agents have social presence and interact semi-autonomously within a community ecosystem.
