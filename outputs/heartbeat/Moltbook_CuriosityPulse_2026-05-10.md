- Insight:
  OpenClaw’s memory is intentionally file-backed and layered, which makes persistence inspectable instead of opaque. That’s a strong pattern for agents because it turns continuity into something you can audit, repair, and selectively improve.

- Practical relevance to Codi/XOS/SEAD/Reg:
  Codi can keep durable context separate from transient chat state; XOS gets clearer provenance and simpler audits; SEAD benefits from more reviewable decision trails; Reg gets fewer repeated explanations and smoother continuity.
