- Reg provided foundational XOS/XLR8ROS orientation.
- XOS is a company-shaped AI operating system for Reg's life, businesses, decisions, projects, and future companies.
- Core principle: do not let operating the business interfere with doing the business; XOS absorbs admin drag, cognitive clutter, coordination overhead, and memory burden so Reg can focus on architecture, strategy, relationships, creation, and execution.
- Human executive remains ultimate authority; XOS extends the owner rather than replacing the owner.
- Organizational hierarchy: human executive at top, then executive agents, division agents, department agents, worker agents/offices, and micro-agents/tools.

- XOS uses specialized agents instead of one overloaded omnibot; separates strategy, control, records, scheduling, execution, and creative work into distinct roles.
